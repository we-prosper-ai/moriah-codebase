## Fundamental #1: Principles & Priorities
*Start by defining what matters most*

### The Core Teaching
> "What We Seek Lies At The Intersection of Fastest Path to Cash and Fastest Path to Freedom."

The fastest path to cash without the fastest path to freedom creates a business that slowly and painfully dies. The fastest path to freedom without cash creates a faster death. Where the two meet — that's maximum leverage within your own values.

### The Filter
When you have clearly defined what you value, you have created a filter for all future decisions. The second you violate your principles and priorities, you become out of alignment with yourself and effectively assure your eventual destruction.

### Who This Is NOT For
> "If your primary driver is making an excessive amount of money at an excessive pace, this is not for you. The hustler mentality destroys families and companies, and I want nothing to do with it."

### The Real Goal
> "7 and 8 figure earners made their money as a means to an end — and that end is freedom and autonomy. They don't want to be subject to other people's dictates. They want to work where they want, when they want, with whom they want."

### Key Truth
> "The systems are made for you, not you for the systems."

### Sarah's Transformation (The Proof of Concept)
An agency owner working 80+ hours a week, flooded with stress every morning. After clarity work: 30 hours a week, doubled revenue, 75% less stress, massages, vacations, time with family. The vision didn't just fix the business — it fixed her life.

### The Roy Disney Principle
> "When your values are clear to you, making decisions becomes easier."

### On Balance
> "The concept of a perfectly balanced life is a myth. You can't do all things greatly at once, but with the right systems and priorities, you can manage what matters most over time."
