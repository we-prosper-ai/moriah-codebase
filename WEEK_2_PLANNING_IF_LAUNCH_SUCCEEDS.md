## IF SOMETHING GOES WRONG IN WEEK 1

**Don't execute this plan.** Instead:

**If signups are slow (< 75 Day 1):**
- Pivot to messaging test (use different subject lines)
- Call top prospects directly (personal sell)
- Delay Week 2 plan, focus on diagnosing

**If onboarding is broken (< 50% completion):**
- Pause new emails mid-week
- Fix product issues Monday-Wednesday
- Resume emails Thursday with improved product
- Adjust Week 2 plan accordingly

**If retention drops (< 30% Day 7):**
- Product isn't delivering value
- Don't scale yet, iterate
- Talk to users about what's missing
- Fix before scaling

**Rule:** Only scale what's working. If it's broken, fix it first.
