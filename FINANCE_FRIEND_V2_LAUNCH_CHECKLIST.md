## IF THINGS GO WRONG

**Users not engaging?**
- Check: Does Email 1 subject line resonate? (Try A/B test)
- Check: Is the app hard to use? (Get feedback)
- Fix: Send Email 2 earlier (day 2 instead of day 3)

**Low email open rate?**
- Problem: Subject line might be off-brand
- Fix: Moriah adjusts based on your feedback
- Test: Try 2-3 subject lines, track which opens best

**Users asking feature X repeatedly?**
- Decision: Build it before v3 launch OR promise it in v3
- Don't: Ignore repeated requests (signals market want)

**User wants refund or to leave?**
- Response: "Thanks for trying. Your feedback helped us. What would make this better for you?"
- Action: Don't fight it. Learn from it.

---

## THINGS TO NOT WORRY ABOUT

❌ **Don't stress about:**
- Perfect email copy (good is good enough)
- Every feature request (you can't build everything)
- One person leaving (you'll lose some, keep most)
- Competition (focus on your users, not others)
- Revenue in month 1 (this is validation, not profit)

✅ **Do focus on:**
- Real user feedback
- Feature requests that repeat
- Users who stay + engage
- What makes them love it
- Building next in response to signals

## Questions Before Launch?

**Q: What if nobody signs up?**  
A: We learn that this audience doesn't want it. We adjust messaging or try different audience. Not a failure, just data.

**Q: What if people hate it?**  
A: We get feedback, iterate on v3 to address concerns. Real feedback is gold.

**Q: What if I don't have 20 warm people?**  
A: Start with 10. We can expand to Tier 2 (influencers) + Tier 3 (community) after.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**  
A: Yes. If v2 isn't working, we pause and focus on v3. No commitment.
