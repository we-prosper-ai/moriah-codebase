## 📋 QUICK ANSWERS (30 seconds)

### "What is Finance Friend?"

Finance Friend is an AI coach that helps you see your spending decisions through a new lens.

**Most finance apps show:** "You spent $500"  
**Finance Friend shows:** "$500, but it's 20 hours + 30% energy cost + less freedom"

It tracks four currencies that actually matter: Time, Energy, Money, Freedom.

That's fundamentally different.

### "I already use YNAB/Mint/Wave. Why switch?"

You probably don't need to.

**When Finance Friend is right for you:**
- You want help deciding before you spend (not tracking after)
- You think about money in terms of trade-offs (time vs. money vs. freedom)
- You want to understand WHY you spend (not just WHERE)
- You want a thinking partner, not a judge

**When Finance Friend isn't necessary:**
- You just want a spending tracker
- You like your current tool and it works
- You need invoicing (use Wave for that)
- You want rigid budgeting rules (YNAB does this better)

Honestly? If you're happy with your tool, keep using it. Finance Friend isn't for everyone.

But if you feel like something's missing from how you think about money, Finance Friend is probably it.

If you're self-employed or managing your own time/energy/money, v3 pays for itself in one good decision per quarter.
