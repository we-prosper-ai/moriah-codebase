## 🚀 Finance Friend's Unique Advantage

### Three Things Nobody Else Does

#### 1. AI Coach (Tina's Voice)
Most apps give you numbers. Finance Friend talks to you.

**Example:**
- Mint: "You spent $450 on dining this month"
- Finance Friend: "You've been spending $450/month on dining. That's 15% of your income. Want to save $50/month there? Here's what it would take..."

**Why it matters:** People need guidance, not just data.

#### 2. Tax Classification Built-In
Accountants charge $200-500 to categorize your spending for taxes. Finance Friend does it with AI.

**Example:**
- YNAB: "You spent $200 at Office Depot"
- Finance Friend: "Is this a business expense? If yes, it's deductible. I've categorized it as Office Supplies (but confirm)."

**Why it matters:** Self-employed people deal with this monthly. Saves hours at tax time.

#### 3. Principles-Aligned Coaching
Not just "maximize savings." But "what matters to you?" and "are you living your principles?"

Uses Tina's Four Currencies framework:
- Time (How much time did you trade for this money?)
- Energy (Was this purchase energizing or draining?)
- Money (Is this worth the cost?)
- Freedom (Does this move you toward freedom?)

**Example:**
- YNAB: "You overspent dining by $100"
- Finance Friend: "You spent $100 on dining. That's worth 4 hours of your time at your rate. Was it worth 4 hours of your life?"

**Why it matters:** People want to make choices aligned with their values, not just budgets.
