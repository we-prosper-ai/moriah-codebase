## Design Notes

### Color Scheme
- Primary: Deep navy (#1a2e5a) — trustworthy, intentional
- Accent: Gold (#d4af37) — value, freedom
- Background: White + light gray
- Text: Dark gray for readability

### Typography
- Headline: Georgia serif (warm, human)
- Body: Inter sans-serif (modern, clean)
- Code/Data: Monospace (clarity)

### Layout
- Mobile-first responsive
- Short sections (attention span)
- Clear CTAs throughout
- Hero image dominates fold
- Trust indicators (badges, testimonials)

### CTA Strategy
- Multiple conversion points (don't make them scroll to bottom)
- Clear value before asking for signup
- Frictionless signup (no credit card)
- Privacy assurance (people worry about banks)

---

## Messaging Strategy

### Core Story
*"You're not bad with money. You just need the right tools and perspective."*

### Voice
- Warm, not corporate
- Honest about problems
- Optimistic about solutions
- Tina's real voice coming through

### What NOT to Do
- Don't shame people about spending
- Don't promise weight-loss-style "quick fix"
- Don't use financial jargon
- Don't make it seem harder than it is
