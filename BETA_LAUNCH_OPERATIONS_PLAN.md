## 💡 Key Success Factors

1. **Speed over perfection** — Launch v2 as-is, improve based on feedback
2. **Warm audience first** — Tina's community is more valuable than cold outreach
3. **Listen obsessively** — The feedback will shape v3 features
4. **Show gratitude** — Beta testers are your future advocates
5. **Share the journey** — Regular updates keep momentum high
6. **Quick iteration** — Weekly small improvements > monthly big updates
7. **Be genuine** — This is Tina's product; let her voice come through

## 🎓 What Could Go Wrong (and Mitigation)

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Users can't upload files | Test with 10 different file formats pre-launch |
| Chat AI gives bad advice | Add disclaimer, include manual review option |
| Performance degrades with users | Load test with 100 concurrent users pre-launch |
| Users expect more features | Clear roadmap communication |
| Support overwhelms team | Auto-responder + FAQ reduces 80% of tickets |
| Pricing is too high | A/B test with beta cohorts, adjust if needed |
| No one signs up | Ensure list quality; personal outreach > email |
