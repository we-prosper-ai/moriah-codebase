## Problem Section

### "The Problem"

Most finance apps feel like spreadsheets with better UX. They're great at math. They're terrible at *you.*

You don't just have a budget. You have a life:
- Invoices that come when you say they come
- A week where you made $8k but feel exhausted
- Tax categories that don't fit the boxes
- Real questions: "Should I raise my rates? Take that project? Take the week off?"

Banks see transactions. You see decisions.

---

## Solution Section

### "What Finance Friend Does"

**1. Understands Your Bank**
Upload a statement or download directly. We see what you earned, what you spent, and what actually matters.

**2. Asks the Right Questions**
Not "where did every dollar go?" but "What's your hourly rate? Are you working enough? Should you be?"

**3. Makes Taxes Painless**
Upload statements. We classify transactions (invoices, expenses, taxes, pay-yourself). Print a report in 10 seconds.

**4. Gives You a Coach**
Ask the app anything: "What's my monthly revenue trend?" "Should I charge more?" "How much should I pay myself?" 

Not an algorithm. A real person's methodology, turned into AI that learns your business.

## FAQ

**Q: Where does my data live?**
A: Your data stays on Finance Friend's servers. Encrypted. Never sold. You can export anytime.

**Q: Can I try free forever?**
A: Yes. You get full tracking forever. You just won't have the coach. Most people upgrade within 2-3 uses because the coaching is that useful.

**Q: Is this YNAB's budgeting system?**
A: No. This is based on real coaching methodology that actually works for freelancers and small business. Different foundation, different approach.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes. No contracts. Cancel in one click. We're not trying to trap you.

**Q: Is my data really safe?**
A: Yes. Encrypted in transit and at rest. SOC 2 compliant. Full audit available if you need it.
