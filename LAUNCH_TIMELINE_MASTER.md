- [ ] Launch with warm audience first
- [ ] Gather feedback daily
- [ ] Be ready to pivot messaging
- [ ] Consider repositioning if needed

---

### Risk 2: v3 Build Takes Longer
**Probability:** Medium  
**Impact:** Medium (delays features)  
**Mitigation:**
- [ ] Architect clearly upfront (done ✅)
- [ ] Estimate conservatively (3-5 days)
- [ ] Parallel track (build while v2 launches)
