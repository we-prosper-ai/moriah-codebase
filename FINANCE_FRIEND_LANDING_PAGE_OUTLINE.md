## 📝 Copywriting Notes

### Tone Rules (Tina's Voice)
1. **Direct:** "Stop trading your life for money" not "Optimize your finances"
2. **Specific:** Use real numbers: "$5K project" not "income opportunities"
3. **Honest:** "It's a trap" not "It's challenging"
4. **Questioning:** "Is this worth it?" not "You should do this"
5. **Empowering:** "Here's how to think" not "Here's what you should do"

### What to Avoid
- ❌ Generic motivation ("You've got this!")
- ❌ Overpromises ("Financial freedom in 30 days")
- ❌ Corporate speak ("Optimize your capital allocation")
- ❌ Lecturing ("Here's how you should budget")
- ❌ Generic testimonials ("Great app!")

### What to Lean Into
- ✅ Real trade-offs
- ✅ The framework (Four Currencies)
- ✅ The pain point (freedom, not just money)
- ✅ The coach's voice (specific, respectful)
- ✅ Specific use cases (side hustlers, solopreneurs)
