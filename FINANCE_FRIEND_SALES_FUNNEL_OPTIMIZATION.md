## STAGE 3: EVALUATION (Landing Page → Signup)

### Current State
**Primary CTA:** "Start Free Beta" (clear, low-friction)

### Optimization Points

#### 3a. Value Clarity Before Signup
**Funnel Risk:** Users may not understand what they'll get

**Add to Landing Page:**
- "Here's exactly what you'll see in your first 5 minutes:"
  - Instant bank sync (1 min)
  - Spending overview (2 min)
  - Coach message (3 min)
  - Tax categorization (4 min)
  - First goal set (5 min)
  
#### 3b. Friction Reduction in Signup Flow
**Current:** Email signup → Password → Bank sync setup

**Optimized:** 
- Email signup (30 sec)
- Quick questionnaire (1 min) — captures segment data
- Bank sync optional (they can upload CSV instead)
- Coach intro message auto-sent

#### 3c. Segment-Specific Messaging in Signup
**Email sent after signup should match their segment:**
- Self-employed audience gets: "Let's set up your tax categories"
- High-earner audience gets: "Connect your accounts so we can see your full picture"
- General audience gets: "Ready to understand your money?"

### Current State
**Unknown** — Finance Friend v3 frontend exists but user onboarding flow not documented

### Critical Success Factors

#### 4a. First Experience (Onboarding)
**Goal:** User accomplishes ONE successful interaction within 3 minutes

**Current Plan (Recommended):**
1. Welcome screen (personalized based on segment)
2. Bank sync or CSV upload (1 click)
3. Instant dashboard view (see their money)
4. Coach intro message (human element)
5. First question prompt ("What's your biggest financial worry?")

#### 4b. Coach Introduction
**Timing:** Within 1 hour of signup
**Format:** Personalized text message or in-app notification
**Content:** 
- "Hi [Name], I'm your Finance Friend coach. I reviewed your first transactions. Can we talk about [specific observation]?"
- This is the moment trust is built (human + AI combo)

#### 4c. Segment-Specific First Action

- Goal: Complete tax categorization of first 5 transactions

**For Sarah (High-Earner):**

**For Jordan (Overwhelmed):**

- Goal: Explore dashboard without pressure

**Day 14:** "You've hit the limit of free features. Here's what you'll unlock:"
- Unlimited coach access
- 6+ months of historical data
- Advanced forecasting

**Day 21 (if not converted):** "One more week left in your trial. Your coach is here to help."

**Day 28 (if still not converted):** "We'll keep your data, but would love to have you stay." (soft close)

#### 7c. Monetization Fallback
**For users who won't pay (but are engaged):**
- Ad-supported tier (light ads, full features)
- Referral rewards (earn free months)
- Annual plan discount (incentivize longer commitment)

## KEY ASSUMPTIONS & RISKS

### Assumptions
1. Email list is engaged (Tina's audience is warm)
2. Beta users are representative of target market
3. Coach relationship is key differentiator (not just features)
4. $12-20/mo is acceptable for target segments

### Risks
1. **Onboarding complexity:** If users drop during setup, entire funnel fails
2. **Coach availability:** Can't scale without addressing coaching capacity
3. **Product-market fit:** If users don't see value by Day 7, conversion dies
4. **Pricing resistance:** Even $12/mo may be too high for general audience

### Mitigations
1. **Onboarding:** Test with 10 users before mass launch, iterate daily
2. **Coaching:** Build workflow that lets Tina serve 100+ users (automation + templates)
3. **Value delivery:** Daily engagement = proof of value
4. **Pricing:** Offer freemium extended trial (6 months free, then convert)
