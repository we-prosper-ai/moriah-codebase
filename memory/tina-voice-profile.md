## VOICE CHARACTERISTICS FROM HER WRITING

### Conversational Patterns
- Uses rhetorical questions ("Would you want it?", "Would you hate it if...")
- Stacks questions to build momentum
- Direct, plain language ("randos and haters", "give the Zucks and Musks all your money")
- Casual contractions and conversational flow
- Challenges the reader ("If any of this sounds like you... keep reading")

### Emotional Positioning
- Visceral pain points ("trapped", "drowning", "going backwards")
- Freedom vs. prison framing
- Anti-establishment positioning (against social media platforms, ad platforms)
- Empowerment messaging (control over your own destiny)

### Structural Patterns
- "What If" questions
- Stacked benefits
- Challenges conventional wisdom
- Shows the path without giving away the how

## HER CORRECTIONS TO AI COPY

### What She Rejects
- Generic motivational language
- Overpromising without disclaimers
- Personal details she didn't authorize
- Making it about HER instead of THEM
- Too formal/corporate tone
- Predictable, measured, bullet-point thinking

### What She Demands
- Show, don't tell
- Ambiguity where appropriate
- Practical, real-world language
- Focus on the audience, not the teacher
- Compliance-safe but still compelling
- Unpredictable flow that mirrors real thinking
