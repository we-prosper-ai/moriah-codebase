## SUPPORT BEST PRACTICES

**Response times:**
- Support emails: < 1 hour (working hours)
- In-app chat: Real-time when possible
- Nights/weekends: Next business day acceptable

**Tone:**
- Friendly, not corporate
- Explain clearly (don't use jargon)
- Acknowledge frustration
- Offer solutions, not excuses

**Follow-up:**
- After 24 hours: "Did that help?"
- After refund: "What would have made this better?"
- After cancellation: "Would you consider coming back if X changed?"

**Escalation:**
- If tech issue: Forward to engineering with details
- If billing: Check account, adjust if needed
- If complex: Offer phone call / video chat
