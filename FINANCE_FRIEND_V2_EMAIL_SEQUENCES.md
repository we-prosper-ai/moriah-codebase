## NOTES FOR TINA

**Why this sequence works:**
1. Personalization (you + them)
2. Value-first (features second)
3. Pacing (not overwhelming)
4. Community (they're co-creators)
5. Urgency (v3 launch, pricing, lifetime access)

**What NOT to do:**
- Don't oversell
- Don't be salesy
- Don't hide the ask
- Don't spam (7 emails over 21 days is sustainable)
- Don't assume they'll remember features (remind them)

**If response is low:**
- Email 1 opened by <40%? Subject line needs work
- Email 2 clicked by <25%? Onboarding is too hard
- Email 3 opened by <40%? They're not engaged enough
- Adjust and re-send if needed
