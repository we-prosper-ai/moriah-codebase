## STYLE GUIDE FOR YOUR RESPONSES

**Do:**
- ✅ Sound like a real person (you)
- ✅ Show you understand their concern
- ✅ Be specific (not generic)
- ✅ Include action steps (what they do next)
- ✅ Be helpful even if you say "no"

**Don't:**
- ❌ Sound like a bot
- ❌ Copy/paste without personalizing
- ❌ Make promises you can't keep
- ❌ Ignore the question (address it directly)
- ❌ Sound defensive

**Example of good response:**
```
Hi Sarah,

You asked about inviting your spouse. I totally get why you'd want that — 
managing finances together is crucial.

Right now it's single-user, but it's on the roadmap for V3 (next quarter).

For now, you could share your login, but it's not ideal.

Want to be in the V3 beta preview? You'd be among first to test multi-user features.

— Tina
```
