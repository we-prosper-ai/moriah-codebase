## Decision Reasoning

### Checkout & Funnels → ThriveCart

This is the clearest decision in the stack. ThriveCart has a **4.8/5** from real users — highest in the category by a wide margin. The reason? **Lifetime pricing.** $495 once, done. No $147/mo ClickFunnels bill that balloons as you scale.

Real user sentiment: "Predictable costs, no surprise increases." That's the standout.

What it does well: checkout pages, one-click upsells, affiliate management, integrations. What it doesn't do: full website builder, advanced design. That's fine — you don't need it for that.

**ClickFunnels 2.0 is not worth it.** 3.9/5 with complaints about slow pages, buggy software, and support lag. The training ecosystem is good but you can get that for free from YouTube.

### Copywriting → Copy.ai

Start on the **free tier** — it's legitimately usable. If you hit limits, $49/mo Pro is the move. Jasper charges $39-59/mo for comparable output quality. Users on both G2 and Reddit agree the AI writing quality gap has closed — what you pay for now is UX and workflow automation, not magic copy.

Copy.ai's **Workflows** feature is where it earns its price: bulk content generation, sequences, templates. Jasper's brand voice compliance is good but at that price point, you're paying for enterprise features you don't need yet.

**Practical note:** For launch copy (sales pages, emails, ads), use Claude/ChatGPT directly and refine manually. Save Copy.ai for volume — social posts, email sequences, A/B variant generation.

### Split Testing → VWO Free Tier

VWO has a solid free starter plan — enough to run A/B tests on landing pages and CTAs without spending anything. 4.3/5 from enterprise users who use the full suite.

The main complaint (page load flicker) matters less on simple landing pages than on complex sites. Start free, see if testing delivers lift, then decide if paid is worth it.

Instapage ($199+/mo) is overkill unless you're running heavy PPC campaigns. Don't buy it yet.

### B2B Outbound → Not Yet

Artisan AI ($500+/mo) is only cost-effective if you're doing systematic B2B outreach at scale. For AntiGravity at this stage, that's not the priority. When you're ready to build an outbound pipeline, revisit this.
