## EMAIL & MESSAGING

### Q: Should I personalize every email or batch send?
**A:** Batch send but stagger 5 minutes apart.
- If you send 150 emails at once: Looks like mass marketing blast
- If you stagger them 5-10 min apart: Looks like personal outreach
- Same effect, better perception

---

### Q: What if someone replies with a hostile question?
**A:** Respond with kindness.
- "Great question. Here's what we thought about that..."
- Don't defend, just explain
- Every negative reply is a chance to convert a skeptic

**Example:**
- **Hostile reply:** "Why should I trust some AI budgeting app?"
- **Your response:** "Fair question. We're not an AI app, we're a budgeting tool with AI helping + human coaching. Difference: You get to talk to a real person (me) about your finances. Try the free beta, see if it feels different."
