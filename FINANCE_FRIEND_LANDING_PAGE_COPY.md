### FAQ Section

**Q: Is my bank information safe?**
A: We don't connect to your bank. You upload your own statement. We never see 
your passwords. Your data stays on our secure servers and is encrypted at rest.

**Q: What file formats do you accept?**
A: PDF and CSV from any bank. If your bank isn't on the list, export to CSV 
and upload that.

**Q: Can I upload multiple months?**
A: Yes. Upload as many statements as you want. Finance Friend learns your 
patterns over time.

**Q: What if I change my mind about a category?**
A: Correct it once and Finance Friend learns. Next month it'll categorize 
similar transactions automatically.

**Q: How does the tax stuff work?**
A: We categorize transactions and flag potential deductions based on tax law. 
You still need a CPA or tax software to file. We just make preparation easier.

**Q: I'm on the Free tier. Can I upgrade anytime?**
A: Yes. Upgrade anytime, no questions asked. Your data goes with you.

**Q: What if I don't like it?**
A: 30-day money back guarantee. No questions asked.

## 🎨 Visual Style (Brief)

- **Color palette:** Navy blue + light gray + green accents (friendly, not sterile)
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif (Inter or similar), generous spacing
- **Images:** Real screenshots, no stock photos, real people in testimonials
- **Tone:** Warm, honest, non-judgmental. Like talking to a trusted friend.
- **Pattern:** Repeat the "judgment-free" and "actually helpful" themes

---

## 🎯 Conversion Elements

1. **Multiple CTAs** — At least 5 places to sign up (hero, after each section, bottom)
2. **Low friction** — No forms. Just email to get access.
3. **Social proof** — Real testimonials (get from beta users)
4. **Scarcity** — "Free through March 31" creates urgency
5. **Value clarity** — Be explicit about what each tier includes
6. **Risk removal** — 30-day guarantee removes purchase anxiety
