# Finance Friend — Refund & Cancellation Policy

**Last Updated:** March 21, 2026  
**Effective Date:** March 22, 2026

---

## 🔄 Refund Policy

### 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you're not completely satisfied with Finance Friend within 30 days of your initial purchase, we'll refund your full payment — **no questions asked**.

**Why?** We're confident in our product, and we want you to be confident too.

---

## 📋 How to Request a Refund

### Step 1: Email Support
Send an email to **support@financefriend.app** with:
- Subject: "Refund Request"
- Your account email
- Purchase date
- Brief reason (optional, but helpful for us to improve)

**Example:**
```
Subject: Refund Request

I purchased Finance Friend on March 22, 2026 (Order #12345).
I'd like to request a refund.

Thanks,
Jane Doe
jane@example.com
```

### Step 2: Confirmation
We'll respond within 24 hours confirming:
- Your refund request has been received
- Amount being refunded
- Expected timeline (5-7 business days)
- Any questions we need answered

### Step 3: Refund Processed
Your refund will be issued to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

---

## 💰 What Gets Refunded

### Full Refunds (Within 30 Days)
✅ Your entire subscription cost  
✅ Any setup fees  
✅ Usage fees (if applicable)

**No proration needed — full refund of what you paid.**

### Partial Refunds (After 30 Days)
If you cancel after 30 days but within your billing month:
- You'll receive a **pro-rated refund** for unused days
- Calculation: (Days remaining / Days in billing period) × Monthly cost

**Example:**
- Subscription: $19.99/month
- You've used 20 days of 30
- Days remaining: 10
- Refund: (10/30) × $19.99 = $6.66

---

## ❌ Non-Refundable Situations

We cannot refund if:

1. **You exceeded the 30-day window** (but we offer pro-rated refunds after)
2. **You used our service to download/export data then cancelled** — refunds cannot be used to get free access to data
3. **Your account was suspended** for violating our Terms of Service
4. **Refund already processed** — only one refund per subscription period
5. **You request a refund > 2 times per year** — this might indicate our product isn't the right fit

**Note:** If you have a legitimate reason that doesn't fit these categories, email support — we'll evaluate case-by-case.

## 🔔 Cancellation vs. Refund

### Cancellation (You Keep Access Until Month Ends)
- Email support@financefriend.app or use Settings → Cancel Subscription
- Your account remains active until the end of your billing month
- You won't be charged again next month
- No refund issued (you paid for the full month)
- Your data stays on your account indefinitely (until you delete it)

### Refund (Get Money Back)
- Request within 30 days of purchase
- Full refund of what you paid
- Account access immediately revoked
- Your data will be deleted within 30 days per our retention policy

---

## 🗑️ Data Deletion

**If you request a refund:**
- Your bank statements and transactions will be deleted within 30 days
- Your account will be deactivated immediately
- This deletion is permanent and cannot be undone

**If you cancel without refund:**
- Your data remains on your account indefinitely
- You can log back in anytime (no new charges)
- You can request data deletion anytime (see below)

**To manually request data deletion:**
- Email support@financefriend.app with "Delete my account"
- We'll delete all your data within 7 days
- You'll receive confirmation when complete

## 🎁 Special Cases

### Group/Team Purchases
- If you purchased multiple seats, each seat must request individually
- We can process group refunds in bulk if requested together

### Credit Card Declines
- If your payment method fails, we'll notify you
- You have 14 days to update payment information before account suspension
- No refund needed — just update your card and continue

### Service Outage Refunds
- If Finance Friend is unavailable for >24 consecutive hours (excluding scheduled maintenance), email support
- We'll provide proportional credit or refund at our discretion
