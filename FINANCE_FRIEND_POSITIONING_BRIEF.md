- Mint (22M users) died March 23, 2024

- **Strict Budgeting Tools** (YNAB) — life-changing but exhausting

## 🏆 Why Finance Friend Wins

### Unique Differentiator #1: Four Currencies Framework

**What competitors do:**
- Show you money
- Maybe add savings goals
- Maybe add budgets

**What Finance Friend does:**
- Shows you the **relationship** between time, energy, money, and freedom
- Teaches you to optimize all four simultaneously
- Surfaces trade-offs before you make them

**Why it matters:**
Most people DON'T fail financially because they can't budget.  
They fail because they're trading **health, relationships, and sanity** for money.

Finance Friend says: "Before you take that gig, let's see what it costs in the other currencies."

**Positioning:** "Not a budgeting tool. A life-optimization tool."

---

### Unique Differentiator #2: AI Coach (Tina's Voice)

**What competitors do:**
- ❌ No chatbot at all (YNAB, Monarch)
- ⚠️ Generic "ask your AI about your spending" (Copilot)

**What Finance Friend does:**
- Coach speaks in Tina's framework
- Proactively surfaces insights ("You earned +$3K but your freedom dropped 50%")
- Uses your actual numbers and principles
- Teaches frameworks instead of prescribing

**Example:**
```
You: "Should I take a consulting gig that pays $5K but needs 60h/week?"

Generic AI coach: "That's $83/hour, which is great income!"

Finance Friend Coach: "That's $83/hour but costs you 20h/week and 40 energy points.
Your freedom score drops from 6/10 to 1/10. Is the money worth that prison?"
```

**Why it matters:**
Users don't want financial advice. They want someone who understands their life.

### Unique Differentiator #3: Tax Classification (Personal Tax Done)

**What competitors do:**
- ❌ YNAB: Nothing (no tax support)
- ❌ Monarch Money: Nothing (pure tracking)
- ❌ Copilot: Nothing
- ⚠️ Wave: Business-only tax features

**What Finance Friend does:**
- Auto-categorizes transactions for personal tax deductions
- Automatically builds quarterly tax report
- Exports directly to tax software (or to CPA)
- Covers side income, capital gains, Schedule C items

**Why it matters:**
If you have ANY side income or investments, you're doing taxes manually or paying an accountant.  
Finance Friend handles it automatically.

**Positioning:** "The only personal finance app where tax prep is automatic."

## 💰 Pricing Positioning

### Why $29/mo is right (not $10 or $50)

**Market positions:**
- Free tier (Mint, before it died): Value problem — free but ad-supported
- $10-12/mo (Credit Karma): Weak commitment — doesn't charge enough to attract serious users
- $15/mo (YNAB, monthly): "Budget app" positioning
- $99+/mo (Copilot, enterprise): "We're for serious investors"

**Finance Friend $29/mo positioning:**
"You're paying for coaching, not just software. Our AI coach is worth $29 alone."

This is:
- Affordable enough for side hustlers ($29 = 2-3 hours of freelance income)
- Expensive enough to signal quality
- High enough margin to support a real coaching product
